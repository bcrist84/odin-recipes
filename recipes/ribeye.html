Smoked Ribeye

Delicious Smoked Ribeye

Welcome steak lovers! If you love steak and want to take it to the next level this is an easy way to do that. For this recipe you will need a way to smoke. A pellet grill, traditional smoker, or a propane grill with a smoke tube. I personally use a pellet grill for this recipe. What we want is low temp and a slower cook. So we can get enough of a smokey flavor added into our steak. Go ahead and choose your choice of wood. We are going to want our temp around 225 degrees.

For the steak I wouldn't go with anything less than an inch and half thick. Because we are smoking the steak, the thicker the steak the longer we will be able to smoke the steak. So with that said time will vary depending on the thickness of the steak. I personally don't go by time, but temp pulling the steak when it is at 115 to 120 degrees. Once the steak hits pulling temp. I sear the steak at a high temp.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2" - 3" Ribeye Steak
  • Coarse Salt
  • Ground Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Salt and pepper ribeye steak (to preference)
  2. Let ribeye steak sit at room temp while setting up grill
  3. Fire up grill to 225 degrees and get your smoke source going
  4. Once your grill is stable and smoking add ribeye steaks
  5. Check ribeye for internal temp every 20 mins til you hit desired temp
  6. When ribeye is at desired temp finish with a sear
  7. Let ribeye rest for 5 - 10 minutes on counter covered loosly by tin foil
  8. Time to eat!
