Smoked Ribeye

Delicious Smoked Ribeye

Welcome steak lovers! If you love steak and want to take it to the next level this is an easy way to do that. For this recipe you will need a way to smoke. A pellet grill, traditional smoker, or a propane grill with a smoke tube. I personally use a pellet grill for this recipes. What we want is low temp and a slower cook. So we can get enough of a smokey flavor added into our steak. Go ahead and choose your choice of wood. We are going to want our temp around 225 degrees.

For the steak I wouldn't go with anything less than an inch and half thick. Because we are smoking the steak, the thicker the steak the longer we will be able to smoke the steak. So with that said time will vary depending on the thickness of the steak. I personally don't go by time, but temp pulling the steak when it is at 115 to 120 degrees.
